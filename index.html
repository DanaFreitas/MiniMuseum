Welcome to Mrs. Mason's Magnificent Mini-Museum
Mrs. Mason's Magnificent Mini-Museum is a spot of education and excitement in the middle of the Idaho Woods. Near Boise, we have educated people of all ages in the wonders of human history, ever since 1920.
A word from Mrs. Mason.
I am Mabel Mason and I am the third generation owner of this museum. Ever since I was a little girl, my two greatest loves have been learning about world history, and appreciating the Idaho landscape. As long as I run this mueseum, I will make sure that anyone else visiting the museum will have the same passions.

Frequently asked questions

Is there parking?

There is plenty for everyone.

Is food allowed in the museum? Can we touch the items?

For the protection of the artifacts, we ask both not to bring food or drinks, and not to touch anything. If you were a bite to eat, we have a cafe attatched nearby.

What does it cost?

There is a small fee of $3 for adults, and it is free for children. This is for the upkeep of the museum and its owners.

Can I bring a service animal?

Yes you can.
